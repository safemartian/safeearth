The Water Project, Inc. is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization unlocking human potential by
providing reliable water projects to communities in sub-Saharan Africa who
suffer needlessly from a
lack of access to clean water and proper sanitation.
For ten years, we have been helping communities gain access to clean, safe water by providing
training, expertise and financial support for water project construction through our staff and
implementing partners.
