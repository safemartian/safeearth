Frequently Asked Questions

Our team are more than happy to answer any questions you may have, most of our discussion takes places in our Telegram Channel if you want to ask us anything personally. Otherwise you can find some frequently asked questions below.

  • How does SafeEarth select the charities who receive donations?

    Charities are selected by the Safe Earth community and are chosen by popular vote. Votes are usually cast on our twitter or telegram channels.

  • How does the transaction tax work?

    Safe earth has a 4% transaction tax. check our the tokenomics section

  • When will you burn more tokens?

    We burned over 40% of the total supply after launch. No more burns are planned currently, but we don't exclude them in the future.

  • What makes SafeEarth different from other coins?

    It's a very broad question. We think our strong points are our transparency, strong work ethic and commitment to donate to charities as much and as often as possible. And of course, as a SafeEarth holder, we basically pay you to hold the token.
